Movement Control

Key Action
Caps Lock Increases camera speed.
A If Orbit Camera toolbar button () is selected:

If camera is rotating clockwise, then stops it.
If camera is stopped, then rotate it counter-clockwise.

If Orbit Camera toolbar button is not selected:

Moves camera to the left.

D If Orbit Camera button () is selected:

If camera is rotating counter-clockwise, then stops it.
If camera is stopped, then rotate it clockwise.

If Orbit Camera button is not selected:

Moves camera to the right.

E Moves camera upwards.
Q Moves camera downwards.
S Moves camera backwards.
W Moves camera forward.
Ctrl+1 Sets camera speed to Slow.
Same as Slow Camera toolbar button ().
Ctrl+2 Sets camera speed to Medium
Same as Medium Speed Camera toolbar button ().
Ctrl+3 Sets camera speed to Fast
Same as Fast Camera toolbar button ().
Ctrl+4 Sets camera speed to Super Fast
Same as Super Fast Camera toolbar button ().
Ctrl+I Cycles through camera speeds:
 Slow (), Medium (), Fast (), Super Fast ()
Ctrl+M Cycles through camera modes:
 Free Camera (), X-locked Camera (), Y-locked Camera (),
 Z-locked Camera (),  Orbit Camera ()
Mouse right
button drag 		Moves the camera in the direction of drag.
Invert Mouse setting reverses the effect of up and down drags.
(accessed via Edit→Preferences menu item.)
Mouse scroll
wheel rotate 		Zooms camera in (when rotating up) and out (down).
Mouse middle
button click 		Moves camera to bring model to center of screen.
Same as Zoom Extents () toolbar button.
